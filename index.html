What is Trigonometry?

Trigonometry studies the relationships between angles and sides of triangles, especially right triangles.

Basic Functions

  • Sine (sin) = opposite ÷ hypotenuse
  • Cosine (cos) = adjacent ÷ hypotenuse
  • Tangent (tan) = opposite ÷ adjacent
