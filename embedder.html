    Update History

    First, you should understand how the site originated, I had entered a public school for the first time Mid-October 2024 and I met with people, soon I saw people mildly frustrated at games getting blocked every day or so, so I made a manually edited HTML file that let you change what you were embedding.

    After awhile, they finally blocked HTML sometime around January 2025, I briefly used W3Schools try it editor, but eventually was like "What about that codepen.io I used when I was 9" and I was right as you could tell.

    Released on Feb 2nd, 2025 - Only a URL entry bar, a load button and fullscreen, lightmode is most accurate to this but no effects existed.

    Eventually after several people knew about it some kids in my History class started using it and I thought that was the end, around Spring 2025?

    I thought it was gonna be blocked but it wasn't, I was surprised, I thought it would be blocked September 2025 but it just kept going.

    An update on September 31st(?) 2025 adds in Quicklinks, styles, settings, and an overall UI update, please contact Omtegu if you know the exact date. Made only because Xander stole my code.

    An update on December 6th 2025 rounds a lot of borders and adds colorcoding to quicklinks, named V1.2.

    Another update named V1.2.1 adds in a new text header that can be modified, and drop shadows on things, along with adding the first 3 Metroid games! - Dec 9th 2025.

    Update 1.2.2 came out later on Dec 9th 2025 fixing rendering of Dark Purple and Dark mode!

    Update 1.2.2.1 came out later that day as well, on December 9th 2025 as well, adding a glow effect on hover. Alongside adding some fancy effects in some other menus.

    Update 1.3 came out on December 10th around 8:30AM. Adding many more effects to the site and reorganizing the layout. Updates continued to release throughout the day to add more effects and layout changes to optimize appearance. An update later that night added a (buggy) low performance mode.
